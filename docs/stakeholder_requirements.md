# Stakeholder Requirements

## Executive Leadership

### Business Questions

- Is revenue growing?
- Which regions generate maximum revenue?
- Which product categories should receive more investment?

### Required KPIs

- Daily Revenue
- Monthly Revenue
- Revenue by Region
- Revenue by Category

---

## Finance Team

### Business Questions

- How much revenue was generated?
- What is the payment success rate?
- What is the average order value?

### Required KPIs

- Daily Revenue
- Average Order Value
- Payment Success Rate
- Refund Amount

---

## Operations Team

### Business Questions

- How quickly are orders delivered?
- Which deliveries are delayed?
- Which sellers are causing delays?

### Required KPIs

- Average Delivery Time
- Late Delivery Percentage
- Delivery Performance by Seller

---

## Product Team

### Business Questions

- Which products sell the most?
- Which categories generate maximum revenue?
- Which products receive poor ratings?

### Required KPIs

- Top Selling Products
- Revenue by Category
- Average Product Rating

---

## Seller Management

### Business Questions

- Which sellers perform best?
- Which sellers require intervention?

### Required KPIs

- Seller Revenue
- Seller Rating
- Cancellation Rate

---

## Customer Experience

### Business Questions

- Are customers returning?
- Are customers satisfied?
- Which customers are at risk of churn?

### Required KPIs

- Repeat Customer Rate
- Customer Lifetime Value
- Average Review Score
